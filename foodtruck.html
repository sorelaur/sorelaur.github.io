Past Story

Generation after generation


Nearly 100 years ago, Germain P. Laurier, Sr. opened Bonbon’s Candy Store in Ghent with a commitment to quality. While the company started as a small family business with a loyal local following, we have now grown into an iconic brand with international distribution. Bonbon’s is proud to delight fans of all ages with scrumptious treats crafted by hand from start to finish.

We think we’re pretty lucky because our staff is family, and many of our customers are now close friends. Bonbon has been helping you to celebrate your most memorable events with sweets and snacks since 1922. According to us, every day is a celebration, and sharing those moments with the people we cherish is priceless. Whether you are ringing in the New Year, or just gathering to make merry. Bonbon has all of the sweet treats you need to make the special occasions of your life exceptional.

Present Story

With the opening of the 3rd store in Ghent we can call ourself a statement in Ghent's surroundings. People from all over the country come to us to see these delicious artisanal sweets. People are found over our natural and diverse taste in candy.

Something brand new in our gamma is chocolate. Like you we are eager to learn about new products. There is nothing freshly made chocolate that had been produced naturaly and fairly. Come and enjoy our recently discovered tastes in chocolates in one of our stores in Ghent.

Future Story

Candy truck on your event


After nearly 100 years it is time that we come to our custumors. With our brand new addition of a candy truck, we are able to provide you a full scala of candy at your event. It is even possible for you to make your own candy at a location/event of your choice. In diverse workshops you are able to learn and produce your own favorite candy.

You don't like to get your hands dirty or you would like people to enjoy what they eat at your event? With our Candy Truck everything is possible! You choose what we bring with us at your party for your guests to enjoy. The choices are chocolate, gummy, sour candy and candy canes. We even produce right in front of your eyes for your entertainment!
