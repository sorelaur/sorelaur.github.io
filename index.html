Spotlights

Chocolat


Crafted in one of Belgium’s oldest cocoa roasteries and made from the best, sustainable cocoa beans of West Africa, Bonbon’s Belgian Chocolate is here for you. To surprise you with world class workablity. And to spoil the taste buds of your fans with the chocolate taste they love so much. Maybe that’s why it’s many chefs n°1 preferred chocolate.

The Belgian Chocolate needs the finest cocoa beans. Today and tomorrow. We believe that we all together, you – chefs – and your customers can all collaborate with cocoa farmers in West Africa – one of the main sources of our high-grade cocoa beans. For every pack of Belgian Chocolate you buy, we reinvest a part in sustainable cocoa farming through the Cocoa Horizons Foundation. This non-profit organisation monitors the most urgent needs in West-Africa and coordinates the sustainable projects we all together contribute to.
